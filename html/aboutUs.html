Around the Block

Mike Morris

Employing Broker / Owner / President

While in the Air Force I moved all over the country. I know what it takes to relocate, and I will do everything I can to make you feel at home in my adopted community of Colorado Springs. I also work with investors ensuring maximum returns on investments. I graduated from Texas A&M with a Bachelor's degree and obtained my MBA while in the Air Force. I have been a Teacher, Airman (enlisted and officer), a First Responder and a Private Contractor. I finally decided to make my real estate hobby into my life's work. I am a proud husband of a wonderful woman and I have three gifted children.
My goal in life is to help families and individuals achieve their dreams. I will get you the best deal. I proudly serve fellow Veterans, First Responders, and Teachers.

Mike Dunlap

Agent

My wife and I moved to Colorado a little over three years ago after deciding to sell our home in Illinois. We wanted to experience all that Colorado has to offer. We are enjoying the weather, the mountains and all of the diversity.
I have always been someone who likes to stay busy, and decided that it was a perfect time to try a different career path. After getting my Bachelor's Degree in Public Relations, I decided to become a tradesman. It was a great way to experience different aspects of the housing industry. Real estate is the avenue that aloows me to use my knowledge and combine it with the experienve that I have had out in the field.
Home improvement has been a passion of mine. I engoy the outdoor, hiking, golfing, and site seeing. Family time is very important to me, making sure to keep balance in my life. The knowledge that I have in home renovations lets me help my clients make the right choices for their needs. I enjoy working with people and seeing them accompish their dreams.
