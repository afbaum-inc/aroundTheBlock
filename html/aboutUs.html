Mike Morris

Employing Broker / Owner / President

While in the Air Force I moved all over the country. I know what it takes to relocate, and I will do everything I can to make you feel at home in my adopted community of Colorado Springs. I also work with investors ensuring maximum returns on investments. I graduated from Texas A&M with a Bachelor's degree and obtained my MBA while in the Air Force. I have been a Teacher, Airman (enlisted and officer), a First Responder and a Private Contractor. I finally decided to make my real estate hobby into my life's work. I am a proud husband of a wonderful woman and I have three gifted children.
My goal in life is to help families and individuals achieve their dreams. I will get you the best deal. I proudly serve fellow Veterans, First Responders, and Teachers.

Agent
